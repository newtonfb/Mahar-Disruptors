The Sky Is The Limit

The Mahar Disruptors are the first state-sanctioned high school esports team in Western Massachusetts. On this site, we will describe our struggles and success and a road map to creating a successful program at your school.

What We Do

Gaming + Education

Esports are co-ed, inclusive to anybody who can use a computer and engage students who might not otherwise participate in extracurricular activities.

Gaming

Video games are a passion for millions of students around the world. Engaging with their passion through esports is a fantastic way to reach students in a space they want to be, while rewarding positive behaviors and skill development. It encourages them to form social bonds and find community while pursuing something they care about.

Teamwork

Every game we play is team-based. Success demands strong, clear communication skills — something highly sought after by every employer.
